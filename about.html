About Me

Brief Introduction

My name is Haningtone Omondi, born in the year 2005 on 7th of July.
Born in Siaya county,Bondo sub-county,West-yimbo location, Got-Agulu sub-location, Uhuaya village, to Carroline Odinga and Elly Masinde

Early Life

At the age of 9 years, I left for my maternal home in Uyoma, Rarieda sub-county where I spent my boyhood life under the care and guidance of my grandparents.

Education Journey

From nursery to class three , I attended Nyayo Primary school back at home before leaving for Lieta Primary School in Uyoma
Having scored 376 marks in the national examinations, I was admitted in Ramba High School in the year 2020
After 4 years of hard work, I came out with grade B+.
Currently, I am a student in Kisii University pursuing Bachelors Degree in Software Engineering in my second year
